OUR VISION

Our children are the future leaders and we as patriotic citizens seek to give these children equal rights and hope as enshrined in the constitution of Ghana.
New Grace Foundation is taking the lead to search for the deprived children and attend to their basic needs. Our facilities are very new and comfortable to provide a good atmosphere for studies and livelihood.   

What we do

Committed to provide education, vocation and welfare to "helpless" and underprivileged children
