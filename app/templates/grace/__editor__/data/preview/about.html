About

New Grace Foundation (NGF) was founded on 18th October, 2016 to help “helpless” and underprivileged children in Ghana. It is charitable, voluntary, humanitarian, non-governmental, non-profit, non-political and nonpartisan organization committed to providing hope, health, education, vocation and security to “helpless” and underprivileged children living in deprived areas in Ghana.
